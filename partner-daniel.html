Daniel is a partner at RJD Associates with over 6 years’ professional experience in Audit and Tax Advisory Services serving clients by providing them with cutting edge business solutions.

Daniel’s experience spans across various sectors including financial services, manufacturing, construction and real estate industry and insurance industry including Non-Governmental Organizations.

Daniel’s professional career started with the SNG Pedabo Ghana where he worked for over (5) five years and rose to the level of Senior Consultant managing over ten(10) clients providing various assurance engagements services, accounting and tax services including tax implications on cross border transactions.

Daniel is a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants Ghana (ICAG), and a Chartered Tax practitioner with the Chartered Institute of Taxation Ghana (CITG) and holds a Master’s degree in Development Finance from the University of Ghana.
